About Me

Hello, I'm David! I am originally from Atlanta, Georgia. I went to college at Georgia Southern University where I majored in Theatre Technologies. After graduation I moved to New York and worked Off-Broadway for a few years. Once Covid hit, I sat around in my apartment for a year and decided I wanted a change but wasn't sure exactly what. I took a year-long job out in Arizona to get back on my feet while I decided what my next step would be. Once that contract was over, I moved back to south Georgia and began a coding bootcamp online with the University of Minnesota. Pivoting my design skills and background from Theatre into web design has been one of the best decisions of my life and I look forward to the future that this new career will provide.
